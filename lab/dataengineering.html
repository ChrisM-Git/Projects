Data Engineering

In this section we will cover what is Data Engineering and take a look at the envrionment. At White Tree, our Data Engineers are responsible for three key areas, they are:

  • Data Mining
  • Big Data
  • Data Pipeline

Data Mining is extracting some knowledge from the data, this can be from documents, csv files or other structured type formats.

Big Data, is allot of data stored in the cloud or on-prem, sometimes also known as a Data Warehouse. In our case, we have lots of pictures of dogs stored within our facility!

Finally there is Data Pipeline, which is the process of getting the data to a format we can use with different tools, techniques
